# LVX Homes — Pilot Capture Guide

*Everything a pilot needs to shoot a property so it reproduces at full quality.
Follow this exactly and the system does the rest — you upload, you get a link.
(Internal processing details live elsewhere; they are not needed to fly.)*

---

## What you're producing

One shoot yields the full LVX package: the interactive 360 flight tour, step-in room
panos, the photoreal dollhouse floorplan with the real aerial around it, and
laser-verified dimensions. Four capture products make that possible:

| # | Capture | Purpose (what breaks without it) |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Nadir video pass(es)** | The floors and the floorplan. Skip a wing → that wing is blank. |
| 2 | **Cinematic 360 flight** | The tour everyone watches. One continuous take, front door to front door. |
| 3 | **Hero 360 still per room** | The step-in panos. No still = no step-in for that room. |
| 4 | **The measurement card** (below) | The truth. Without it, nothing is dimensioned — only pretty. |

## Camera settings (do NOT improvise)

- 360 camera: **Full-Sphere, highest resolution**, HorizonSteady/heading-stabilization **ON**.
- **Video Subtitles / flight-data logging ON.** Record 10 s, land, CONFIRM a `.SRT` file sits
  beside the clip. **No SRT = the shoot cannot be scaled. Verify every shoot, every day.**
- Stills: full-resolution 360 panos, camera near room center, ~chest-to-eye height.

## Flight patterns

**Nadir pass(es)** — the floor mapper:
- Fly *every room* of a wing in one continuous take, ~4–6 ft altitude, slow (walking pace),
  camera path through room centers *and along walls* — hug within ~3 ft of each wall run once.
- Big house → one pass per wing is fine, **but every pass MUST share one large room with
  another pass** (fly through the kitchen/great room in both). That shared room is how the
  wings join — no overlap, no join.
- Enter every enclosed space: closets, pantries, laundry. Open every door before takeoff.

**Cinematic flight** — the tour:
- One continuous take: street → driveway → front door → every room in a natural walking
  order → backyard/pool → return → climb-out. Pause 2–3 s in each room's center.
- Cross thresholds slowly and level. The doorway needs visible texture on BOTH sides
  (doormat outside, furniture inside) — a bare threshold kills the take.
- Screen doors OFF or fully out of the flight path.

**Hero stills** — the step-ins:
- One 360 still per room, TWO in large/open rooms. Stand-height, room center,
  *inside* the room (a closet shot from its doorway is not a closet capture).
- Baths: door open but out of frame-swing; be aware mirrors read as fake depth — stand
  where the mirror doesn't dominate.

## The measurement card (the "bare handful of truth")

Ten minutes with a laser measure. This is what turns imagery into dimensions:

1. **Ceiling height in each ceiling zone** (main plateau + any lower wing) — 1 number each.
2. **Two wall spans per key room, roughly perpendicular** (e.g., great room north wall +
   east wall), 4–6 rooms minimum spread across the house.
3. **One doorway wall thickness** (jamb depth).
4. **Photo of the builder/assessor floorplan** if one exists — flat, all edges in frame.

Write them on the room or enter them in the Studio Measure sheet on-site. More spans =
tighter verification; the minimum above is enough to dimension the whole property.

## Upload (Studio)

1. `lvxhomes.com/studio/projects` → New → property address; note the slug.
2. Upload with the right role — this is what triggers processing:
   - Cine flight → **Video** · Nadir passes → **Nadir** · Hero stills → **Still**
   - `.SRT` files → **Telemetry** (one per flight — do not skip)
   - Plan photo → **Still** named `plan-…`
3. Enter the measurement card in the Measure sheet (or hand it to ops).
4. Submit. Processing starts automatically; ops reviews and publishes. You'll get the
   hidden preview link — nothing is public until approved.

## Pre-flight checklist (print this)

- [ ] SRT test clip verified on THIS camera TODAY
- [ ] Stabilization ON, full sphere, max res
- [ ] Every interior door open; screen doors cleared; blinds up (light matters)
- [ ] Doorway texture both sides of the main threshold
- [ ] Nadir: every room, wall-hugs, wing overlap room flown in both passes
- [ ] Cine: one continuous take, 2–3 s pause per room, slow thresholds
- [ ] Stills: every room from INSIDE it, 2 in big rooms
- [ ] Measurement card complete (ceilings, spans, jamb, plan photo)
- [ ] Uploads role-tagged, SRTs attached, measurements entered

## Quality tiers (what the minimum buys)

- Video + SRT only → tour, un-dimensioned floors ("pretty, not measured").
- \+ measurement card → **dimensioned** floorplan, verified scale (our standard).
- \+ plan photo → plan-hybrid dollhouse (design lines + photo floors).
- \+ second nadir per wing / extra spans → tighter coverage + tighter verification.
